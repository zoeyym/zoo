About Our Zoo!

Established in 1992, our zoo has been making families happy for 32 years! It all started when two best friends came up with this crazy idea, never knowing where it would bring them today. As the years went on, more vistors wanted to explore our attractions, and learn more about our exotic animals. As our establishment grew, our love for animals did too. Today, the two childhood best friends always ensure the animals have the safest and most comfortable homes!

Our diverse zoo holds hundreds of animals, all with different backgrounds, habitats, diets, and more! Take a new adventure each time you explore one of our ten exhibits! As of today, we are always brainstorming new habitats for our animals and new activities for our visitors. When you visit, please remember to respect the animals because this is their home too!
