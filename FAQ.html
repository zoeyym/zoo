Frequently Asked Questions


When was this establishment opened?

Adventure Zoo was built in 1982, and open to the public in the summer of 1985!

How do I navigate me and/or my family around the zoo?

At the entrance, there are free paper maps. There is also a QR Code to scan that gives a mobile version of the map!

What is the best age to bring my children?

At Adventure Zoo, we recommend as early as 1.5 years because there are products and activities to help!

Are all of the animals there all year round?

The answer is it depends! Some of our animals stay the entire year while some are temporarily there due to weather, health, or construction!

What times are you open?

We are open Mon-Sat, from 8:30 AM to 6 PM through March, up until October! We stay open later on days we have special events, that you can check out on our services page!
